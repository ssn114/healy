Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Healy (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it.

What information we collect

We only collect your email address when you choose to sign up for our waitlist.

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, or any other personal information.

Why we collect your email

We collect your email address only to:

  • Notify you when Healy launches
  • Share important updates related to the launch

We do not send marketing emails, promotions, or advertisements at this stage.

How your information is stored

Your email address is securely stored using ConvertKit, an email service provider that helps us manage our waitlist.

ConvertKit stores your data on secure servers and is required to handle your information responsibly.

How we use your information

We use your email address only to:

  • Send launch or waitlist-related updates
  • Communicate information you have explicitly signed up to receive

We do not sell, rent, or trade your email address.

Sharing your information

We do not share your personal information with third parties.

The only exception is our use of ConvertKit to store and send emails on our behalf.

Unsubscribing

You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the “unsubscribe” link in any email we send you.

Once you unsubscribe, you will no longer receive emails from us.

Cookies and tracking

Healy does not use cookies, analytics tools, or tracking technologies at this time.

If this changes in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally.

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us via our official social media channels.
